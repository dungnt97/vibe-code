## Q1 2026 Overview

Ethereum has demonstrated strong performance in the first quarter of 2026, driven by increasing Layer 2 adoption and growing institutional interest. Let's examine the key trends and what they may signal for the months ahead.

## Price Action

ETH opened Q1 at approximately $3,800 and has shown a steady upward trajectory. Key support and resistance levels have been established:

- **Support**: $3,600 (strong), $3,200 (major)
- **Resistance**: $4,200 (immediate), $4,800 (psychological)

## On-Chain Metrics

### Network Activity

Daily active addresses have increased by 25% compared to Q4 2025, indicating growing network usage. Gas fees have remained relatively stable thanks to continued Layer 2 scaling.

### Staking

The total amount of ETH staked has reached new highs, with over 35 million ETH locked in the Beacon Chain. This represents a significant portion of the total supply and contributes to reduced selling pressure.

## Outlook

Based on current trends, we maintain a cautiously optimistic outlook for Ethereum in Q2 2026. Key factors to watch include regulatory developments, Layer 2 ecosystem growth, and broader macroeconomic conditions.
